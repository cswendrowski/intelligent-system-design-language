Who are ISDL users designing sheets for?

This determines how opinionated the default styling should be

A

Mostly Themselves

Building a sheet for their own game or table. Functional matters more than polish.

B

Publishing for Others

Want their sheet to look polished for a community audience. First impressions matter.

C

Both Roughly Equally

A mix of personal projects and community-facing systems. Needs to work for both.

D

New-to-Dev TTRPG Creators

People who need great defaults out of the box without writing CSS. The tool does the design work for them.
