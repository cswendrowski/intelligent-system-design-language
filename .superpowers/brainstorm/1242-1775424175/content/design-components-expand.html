Expand Pattern Rules

Click to expand

Click the bar or a small chevron. Expansion is inline — it pushes content below down, not an overlay. Same container grows taller.

Which fields expand?

Resources (value/max/temp), trackers with editable max, money with denominations, measured templates. Simple fields (string, number, boolean) don't need expansion — they're directly editable inline.
